The Cycle

What happens inside the dock

Four automated stages run back to back, entirely on-device — this is the real order the hardware follows, so we number it like a process rather than a feature list.

01
📸

Smart 3D Scanning

Rest your hand on the pink surface and slide in. Micro-cameras in each port measure the size, curve and thickness of every nail in real time.

02
✨

Nano-Polishing

Vibrating nano-glass pads trace each nail's natural curve for a mirror shine. Pressure sensors auto-soften for thin or sensitive nails.

03
🌿

Spa Aromatherapy Mist

A warm lavender-eucalyptus scent fills the air while a micro-mist of Vitamin E, keratin and argan oil nourishes each nail bed.

04
💠

UV Flash & Self-Clean

A 3-second built-in UV-C flash clears 99.9% of bacteria and dust from every port, so the dock is ready for the next hand instantly.

Why It's Different

Built small on purpose

BEURER borrows its engineering philosophy from foldable phones, not salon equipment — which is what lets a full manicure station sit next to your keyboard.

📦 Pocket-Sized Robotics

Instead of bulky motors, BEURER uses the same micro-components found in foldable smartphones, keeping the whole dock slim, flat and light enough for a desk corner.

📶 Smart App Connection

Paired over Bluetooth, the companion app logs your nail health over time and lets you choose spa scents or treatment intensity before every session.

🛡️ Adaptive Safety Sensors

If a nail is thin or sensitive, pressure is automatically reduced mid-cycle — the same protective logic across all five ports, independently.

♻️ Self-Cleaning Between Uses

The built-in UV-C flash means the dock is hygienic for the next person the moment your cycle ends — no manual sanitizing step.
